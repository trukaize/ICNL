About ICNL

What is goal of ICNL?

Intelligent Computing & Networking Laboratory (or ICNL), led by Professor Jeongho Kwak, is with the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (or EECS) and Department of Artificial Intelligence (AI) Daegu Gyeongbuk Institute of Science and Technology (or DGIST). ICNL's research focus lies in developing algorithmic and practical solutions of important networking problems, their performance and analysis over cloud computing and wireless networks. Recent research efforts in ICNL have been quite diverse, ranging over (compute/storage/network) resource orchestration, state-of-the-art 6G communications, and AI for mobile/edge/cloud computing architecture.
