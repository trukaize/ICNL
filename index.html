Research Topics

Cloud/Edge Computing

We aim at developing essential technologies in cloud/edge computing architecture via compute/storage/network resource orchestration.

  • Find out more

MEC-Assisted On-device AI

We address MEC (Mobile Edge Computing)-assisted On-device AI in mobile systems leveraging optimization theory, federated learning and reinforcement learning.

  • Find out more

5G+/6G Communications

We aim at developing radio resource management techniques in 5G+/6G communication networks for satellite networks (Starlink) and autonomous vehicles (V2X).

  • Find out more
